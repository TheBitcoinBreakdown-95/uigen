Basking Ridge Garden Club

Organized 1923 | Federated 1925

The purpose of The Basking Ridge Garden Club is to encourage the spirit of gardening, to preserve our trees, birds and wildflowers, to be environmentally aware, and to contribute to the beautification of our community.

We Meet Monthly

Our monthly meetings are scheduled the first Monday of the month and take place at the Environmental Education Center located at 190 Lord Stirling Road, Basking Ridge

9:15am for refreshments

10:00am Business Meeting

10:45am Program

We Love to Dig!

We are an active group and like to get our hands dirty!

You can often see us working in town, at the VNA of Somerset Hills, the Bernards Township Library and the Environmental Education Center, to name a few.

Apply for Membership

Join our vibrant community of garden enthusiasts! We welcome members who share our passion for gardening, environmental stewardship, and community beautification.
